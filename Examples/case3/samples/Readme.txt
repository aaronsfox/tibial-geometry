Imperial College London femur and tibia surface mesh set
========================================================
(Tsang, C.K., Zhang, K., Nolte, D., Kedgley, A.E., Bull, A.M.J.;
Department of Bioengineering, Imperial College London, UK)

The dataset contains bone geometries of the left and right thigh (femur) and 
shank (tibia and fibula) segmented from magnetic resonance (MR) scans of 35 
healthy volunteers (22 male, 13 female; height 155 cm to 193 cm, weight 
45 kg to 108 kg, body mass index 17.0 kg/m2 to 34.8 kg/m2).


If you are using this dataset, please cite:

Nolte, D., Tsang, C.K., Zhang, K., Ding, Z., Kedgley, A.E., Bull, A.M.J., 2016. 
"Non-linear scaling of a musculoskeletal model of the lower limb using statistical 
shape models". J. Biomech. 49, 3576–3581. doi:10.1016/j.jbiomech.2016.09.005
